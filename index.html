Frequently Asked Questions

  • Q: Is there light and dark mode?

    A: Yes, it is adjusted automatically based on your system preference.

  • Q: Do I have to keep the app open for the timer to keep going?

    A: Yes, the idea is to occupy your phone with something else so that you can't use it. We will be adding more convenient features around this in the future.
